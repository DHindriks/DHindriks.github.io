Space Arena

An arcade shooter where the player takes control of a small fighter ship, the goal is to get the highest score and survive as long as possible.


Gameplay

The player can see the highest score and the name of the player who made it in the main menu.

The game itself takes place near an enemy space station, the debris around the station can be used as cover against enemy fire.
Debris can be pushed too, destroying an enemy with debris will award double points, but pushing debris will damage the player as well.
