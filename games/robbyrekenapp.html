Robby's Rekenapp

A math game for android, made in Unity.


My first Unity project, a simple 2d math game.
Players need to be quick, and tap on the right answers before they float off screen.
If the player gets a wrong answer or takes too long, the player will lose a life, losing all 5 lives will result in a "game over".
getting a certain amount of right answers will result in a "win".
