Robby's Rekenapp

A math game for android, made in Unity.


My first Unity project, a simple 2d math game.
Players need to be quick, and tap on the right answers before they float off screen.

The player loses a live when an incorrect answer was given or when the player takes too long.
When all 5 lives are lost, the player will be send to a game over screen.
The player wins when enough correct answers were given, the amount of answers depend on the level.

Robby's RekenApp can also be found on Google Play.
