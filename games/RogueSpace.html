Rogue Space

a top down roguelike where the player explores a sector in space and the derelict spaceships within.


Gameplay

Loot and equipment

In the world the player explores, the player can find a lot of different items and equipment. There are 3 types of equipment in this game: weapons to deal damage with, shields that help with defense and engines that that improves the player's movement.
There are different types of items and equipment and they can be acquired in different ways, some items can only be found in certain locations, while others can only drop from certain enemies.


Exploration

In this game, the player explores a randomly generated world, many of the structures the player can find also have randomly generated interiors.
