Plastic Catastrophe

A location based augmented reality game for mobile devices. The goal is to help the local animals clean up the area, making them stronger and healthier.


Gameplay

As the player walks around in real life, the character in the game will follow using the device's GPS, trash will randomly spawn around the player character and near water, picking up this trash will help keep the environment and animals clean and healthy.


Characters and minigames

There are 3 different animal species in the game that the player can switch between: the turtle, seal and seagull.
Each character has its own minigame that the player needs to complete in order to pick up trash items. Players will earn the trash item and XP points for the selected animal by successfully completing the minigames.
switching between the different animals is encouraged, animals will gain more XP from picking up trash that is dangerous to their species. Each animal also has limited amounts of energy, characters with low energy can't pick up more trash and will need time to rest.
