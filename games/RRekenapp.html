Robby's rekenapp

One of my first Unity projects, a small game to practice math on Android devices.


Gameplay

There are 3 levels in the game, all levels function the same but have a different theme.
at the start of a level, a randomly generated sum will appear on screen, shortly after, five answers will fly from one side of the screen to the other.
At least one of these answers is correct, the player needs to tap on that answer before it goes off-screen again.
If the player runs out of time or taps on te wrong answer, one of the 5 lives will be lost, the loses the level when there are no lives left.
The player needs to answer around 15 sums correctly to win the level, the player will move further in the level after answering a few sums correctly.

Levels

There are 3 levels in the game: sky, water and space, after every 3 sums correctly answered, the camera moves further up or down depending on the level.
The difficulty is the same in each level, the only differences are the backgrounds and the interactable objects.
