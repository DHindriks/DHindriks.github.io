BlockSwap

A puzzle game where the player rolls blocks around to get to the finish.

Blockswap can be found on Github here.


Gameplay

The player starts each level with the amount of blocks needed to complete the level.
One of these blocks is made out of gold, this block needs to touch the finish tile to finish the level.


Controls

The game is made to be played on a custom controller, using a Teensy board and a gyroscope, the player can tilt the controller to roll the blocks.
Only one block can be controlled at a time, the player can press a button to switch between the blocks.


Obstacles and puzzles

Blocks can be placed to act as a bridge for other blocks, they can also be placed on buttons to make certain things happen. For most levels, there are multiple routes to reach the finish.
